# 🎨 Design Patent Event Website

A simple website created for a **Design Patent Event** conducted by the MCA Department of Marian College Kuttikkanam.

---

## 📌 About

This website provides information and registration access for students to participate in the event.

Participants can submit their ideas, and selected designs may be patented **for free**.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

* Share event details
* Enable easy registration
* Encourage students to bring innovative ideas

---

## 🌐 Features

* Event information
* Registration access
* Simple and user-friendly design

---

## 🚀 Note

This initiative aims to support student creativity and innovation by helping them turn ideas into patented designs.
